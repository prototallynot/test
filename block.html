HOW TO PLAY

The goal is to arrange the falling blocks (tetrominoes) to complete horizontal lines. When a line is complete, it disappears and you earn points. The game gets faster as you level up!

  • Complete lines to earn points and level up
  • More lines at once give more points (Tetris = 4 lines)
  • Game speeds up with each level
  • Game ends when blocks stack to the top
